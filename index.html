Product range

Common cable tray types for industrial and commercial projects

Choose from common cable tray designs for power distribution, data cabling, industrial plants and commercial building projects.

Perforated Cable Tray

Ventilated metal tray for power and control cables in factories, data rooms and building services.

  • Width: 50-1000 mm
  • Height: 25-200 mm
  • Thickness: 0.8-2.5 mm

Ladder Cable Tray

High load capacity structure for long span cable runs, heavy duty power distribution and plant rooms.

  • Span: 2-6 m options
  • Side rail: formed steel
  • Accessories: bend, tee, reducer

Wire Mesh Cable Tray

Open basket tray for quick installation, flexible routing and excellent airflow around low voltage cables.

  • Finish: zinc plated or SS
  • Fast cutting on site
  • Suitable for data cabling

Solid Bottom Tray & Cover

Closed cable containment where extra protection from dust, falling objects or visual exposure is required.

  • Snap-on or bolted cover
  • Indoor and outdoor use
  • Color matching available

Buyer questions

Cable tray export FAQ

These answers help buyers prepare drawings, specifications and quotation details before contacting our sales team.

What cable tray types can you quote?

We can quote perforated cable tray, ladder cable tray, wire mesh cable tray, solid bottom tray, covers, bends, tees, reducers, brackets and related accessories.

Which finishes are available?

Common finishes include pre-galvanized, hot-dip galvanized, electro zinc, stainless steel and powder coated options. The right finish depends on the installation environment and project specification.

What information is needed for an RFQ?

Please send tray type, width, height, length, material, finish, quantity, destination port and drawings if available. This helps us prepare a faster and clearer quotation.

Can you support OEM sizes and packing?

OEM dimensions, labels, bundle packing, pallet packing and mixed-size orders can be discussed based on project drawings and order quantity.
