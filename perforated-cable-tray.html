FAQ

Perforated cable tray questions

Is perforated tray suitable for outdoor use?

Yes, with the right finish. Hot-dip galvanized or stainless steel options are commonly discussed for outdoor or corrosive environments.

Can covers be supplied?

Covers, bends, tees, reducers and mounting accessories can be quoted together with the tray.

Can you make custom holes?

Non-standard punched holes can be discussed when drawings or samples are provided.

How should I compare prices?

Compare material, thickness, zinc layer or finish, tray dimensions, packing method and accessory list, not only the unit price.
