FAQ

Ladder cable tray questions

When should I choose ladder tray?

Choose ladder tray when the project needs stronger support, good ventilation and easier cable fixing for heavy power cables.

Can covers be added?

Yes, covers and accessories can be quoted with the ladder tray according to the drawing and installation route.

Which finish is common for export?

Pre-galvanized, hot-dip galvanized and powder coated finishes are commonly requested, depending on the project environment.

Can you quote by drawing?

Yes. Drawings are preferred for ladder tray projects because side rail, rung spacing and accessories affect the quotation.
