FAQ

Wire mesh cable tray questions

What is wire mesh tray best for?

It is often selected for data cabling, low voltage wiring and routes where flexibility and airflow are important.

Can it be cut on site?

Wire mesh tray is commonly adjusted on site by installers, which is one reason it is used for flexible routing.

Which finish should I choose?

Electro zinc is common for indoor use, while hot-dip galvanized or stainless steel may be discussed for demanding environments.

Can accessories be supplied?

Connectors, supports, brackets, dividers and mounting hardware can be quoted with the tray.
